ABOUT THE PROJECT

This class project was created by a group of students for DTC 475 Digital Diversity at Washington State University Vancouver. The intention of this project was to create a website that informs users about the topic of housing insecurity among college by sharing causes, effects, and personal stories.

The intention of this website was to go beyond just sharing information by also providing information and directions resources that can be utilized by people visiting and engaging with the site. This is a website that pools a bunch of resources on one site so that users have one location to find possibly all the resources they need in one location. Housing and food insecurity is something millions of people face every day. It is important to have information accessible and available to all people. Our website functions to fill that purpose.
